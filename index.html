Services

Chesnut Land Surveying primarily provides boundary surveys, retracements surveys, property line staking, and property corner location.

Possible scenarios we can help with:

  • Where are my property corners or property lines?
  • I have a loan closing or re-finance coming up on my home in a subdivision.
  • I’m purchasing a lot/house in a recorded subdivision.
  • I’m purchasing a larger tract of land that hasn’t been subdivided in the past.

Should your particular concern not be listed, please connect with us for a discussion about your needs. I am more than happy to help!
