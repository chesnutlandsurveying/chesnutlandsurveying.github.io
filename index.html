Services

Chesnut Land Surveying primarily provides boundary surveys and property line staking.

I predominantly serve the following counties/areas: Cheatham, Davidson, Dickson, Maury, Robertson, Rutherford, Sumner, Williamson, and Wilson.


A boundary survey is more than just measuring property lines and marking corners. It involves

  • thorough research of historical records,
  • careful verification of boundaries (including neighbors'),
  • precise measurements using professional-grade equipment,
  • and time in the office to draft and finalize the survey map.
By investing in a detailed survey, you ensure that your property lines are accurate and legally defendable, protecting you from potential disputes or costly mistakes in the future. My approach prioritizes accuracy and compliance with professional standards, ensuring long-term peace of mind.


Possible scenarios I can help with:

  • Where are my property corners or property lines?
  • I’m purchasing a lot/house and want to be sure I know what I'm buying.
  • The city is requiring a Site Plan for an addition.
  • I need to resolve a dispute with a neighbor over property lines.
  • I’m planning to build a fence or other structure.
  • The city or county requires a survey for a permit.
  • I need a topographic survey for a new development project.
